Introduction

Visualisazion is key in efficiently communicating science to peers, politics, and the public. Crafting effective visualisations requires hands-on experience with existing tools, concepts, and methods.

The GEOMAR Visualisation Atlas is the result of an interactive lecture series on proven and upcoming data-driven visualisation techniques and topics - by and for ocean researchers at GEOMAR. In additional two external key note speakers were invited for additional contributions to the GEOMAR Visualisation Week 2022.

The Visualisation Atlas includes multiple perspectives on scientific visualisation from different ocean research disciplines, as well as hands-on advice. It consists of 20 written sections, spanning different topics related to visualisation in ocean research, and written by experts from different disciplines. Each section is accompanied by a video lecture.
