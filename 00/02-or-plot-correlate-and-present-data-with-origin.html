Overview

Plot, correlate, and present data with Origin

Julie Belo, Volcanologist

What does the graphic show?:

XY- plot featuring major element glass compositions to do geochemical finger-printing. Correlation fields for terrestrial volcanic deposits and average compositions of marine tephra layers indicate the chemical similarities.

Personal perspective:

Correlation plots help to find geochemical overlaps of different deposits.

How was this visualization created?:

Excel --> Origin --> Corel Draw
