ARENA2: Collaborative, Interactive & Immersive Visualization

Tom Kwasnitschka, Lead, Seafloor Visualization Lab

What does the graphic show?:

Interpretation of large-scale bathymetric datasets profits from the insight of groups of scientists. At the ARENA2, such data sets can be explored interactively and in an immersive way. This creates a sense of physical presence, sparking holistic, or even totally new and emergent scientific approaches.

Personal perspective:

Visualization is both a means to gain insight and to communicate this insight to others. Ideally, both effects happen at the same time, as in the scene above. The outcome of such sessions is hard to predict and the benefit is often not easy to quantify. Nevertheless, if seriously and consistently applied, methods like this improve the corporate culture of working groups and increase overall productivity.

How was this visualization created?:

ARENA2 is a projection dome of 6m diameter with 5 seamlessly blended video channels fed by a video server array and a computer cluster that can be swapped at runtime. Real time visualization can come from both systems.
