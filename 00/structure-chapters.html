Structure and Chapters

This Visualisation Atlas covers diverse aspects of visualisation in ocean research.

The four main covered topics are:

• domain applications

spanning various visualisation programms and pipelines in physical oceanography, geology/chemistry, marine biogeochemistry, and biology.

• interactivity

spanning multiple platforms and tricks for interactive data visualisation.

• productivity & workflows

spanning fundamental knowledge on data visualisation to advanced tools, programs, and workflows for reproducible science.

• knowledge transfer

spanning examples of data visualisations used for knowledge transfer for internal and public communication, as well as for preparation of research proposals.
