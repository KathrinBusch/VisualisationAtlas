Overview

Interactive and reproducible visualization of ‘omics data

A. Murat Eren (Meren), Microbial Ecologist, Computer Scientist, Head of Ecosystem Data Science at HIFMB

What does the graphic show?:

The figure demonstrates an integrated strategy for interactive visualization of multiple ‘omics approaches to investigate the ecology and evolution of a marine microbial clade, SAR11.

Personal perspective:

Integrated visualization offers critical means to communicate associations between independent aspects of complex datasets. Our ability to ‘recognize’ interesting patterns in increasingly complex datasets and ‘show’ it to others dramatically benefits from detailed visualizations as well as interactive interfaces to interact with it for exploratory analyses.

How was this visualization created?:

Primary visuals were generated by anvi’o, and polished in Inkscape.
