Overview

Visualisation of ocean velocities from high-resolution model data in python

Tobias Schulzki , Ocean modeler

What does the graphic show?:

The visualisation shows a snapshot of the modelled surface velocity, depicting the coherent Gulf Stream and its break-up into mesoscale ocean eddies after separating from the US coast.

Personal perspective:

This visualisation gives a comprehensive impression of the output and performance of high-resolution ocean models, showing the presence of narrow boundary currents and a rich eddy field. Such visualisations help to identify general characteristics of the circulation and are well suited as an overview figure and to introduce a study region.

How was this visualization created?:

The visualisation was created using the matplotlib and cartopy python libraries. To document the process of generating visualisations we use jupyter notebooks.
