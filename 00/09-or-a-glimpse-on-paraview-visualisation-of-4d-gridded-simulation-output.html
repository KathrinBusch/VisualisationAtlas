Overview

A glimpse on ParaView - visualisation of 4D gridded simulation output

Klaus Getzlaff, Data Steward within Research Data Management

What does the graphic show?:

Sea Surface Temperature (5-daily average) projected on Sea Surface Height overlayed with Sea Ice coverage from the high-resolution VIKING20X simulation for the period 2012 to 2018 as a contribution to an interview for the ZDF Mittagsmagazin on the 8th of July 2019.

Personal perspective:

This visualization technique is used in many disciplines and can be used to produce state-of-the-art visualizations for outreach, stakeholders but also for science purpose.

How was this visualization created?:

This visualization was created using the open-source software ParaView, please find a simple demo git-repository here: https://git.geomar.de/visualisation_general/ParaView_demo

The script producing the above mentioned visualization will be available on request, please contact kgetzlaff@geomar.de
