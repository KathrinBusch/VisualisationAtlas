AUV POSEIDON: Visualisation of CAD data and the use for public and internal communication

Christoph Kersten, Graphic Designer

What does the graphic show?:

3D-renderings for a non-scientific poster and a GEOMAR webpage.

Personal perspective:

Using still and animated CAD data is a visual eyecatcher and a good tool for public communication to illustrate and transfer technical/scientific content.

How was this visualization created?:

InDesign, Photoshop, TYPO3 CMS-Backend
